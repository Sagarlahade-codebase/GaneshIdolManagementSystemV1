About Us

This Web Application is about ganesh idol immersion and effect of that on environment and providing analysis and solution for idol immersion managemant
This portal is to track the idol immersion process from its make, by artesian till its purchase by buyer using bar code and registration. Local authorities and NGOs can track and collect the non-disposable idols and reuse them instead of immersing in local water bodies.
